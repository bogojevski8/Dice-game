How to play

Player number 1 starts the game. When the button "Roll dice" is clicked a random dice from numbers 1-6 will appear and the number will be saved into the current score. If the dice is number 1 then the saved score is lost and it's the next player's turn. The hold button is used to store the current score into the final one. However, when you click on the "Hold" button it switches to the other player. For a player to win, one has to have a score of 20. The "New game" button resets the scores and the game starts from the beginning. Good luck!
